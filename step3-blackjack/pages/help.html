How to Play Blackjack

Objective

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Card Values

  • ♦ Cards 2â€“10 are worth their face value
  • ♣ Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10
  • ♥ Aces are worth 1 or 11 (whichever works best for your hand)

Gameplay

  1. Place your bet using the chips at the bottom
  2. You and the dealer will each receive two cards
  3. One of the dealer's cards remains face down
  4. Choose to Hit, Stand, or Double Down to play your hand
  5. The dealer will play their hand according to fixed rules

Player Actions

  • Hit: Take another card
  • Stand: End your turn with your current hand
  • Double Down: Double your bet and receive exactly one more card

Strategy Recommendations

The recommendation system helps you make optimal decisions based on basic blackjack strategy. This is a mathematically calculated approach that gives you the best odds against the house.

To use recommendations:

  • Click the lightbulb icon on the left side of the table to toggle recommendations on/off
  • When enabled, you'll see a suggestion bubble showing the statistically best move
  • The recommended action button will also be highlighted
  • Recommendations update automatically after each new card is dealt
  • You can also enable/disable recommendations in the Settings menu

Following basic strategy won't guarantee wins on every hand, but it optimizes your chances over time.

Note: When you have 21, the system will always recommend Stand (and prevent Hit/Double).
